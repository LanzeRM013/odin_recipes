Simple Baked Chicken Breast

Back to Homepage

A tried-and-true recipe for baked chicken breast that is universally loved, while being incredibly easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Creole seasonings

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and pre-heat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Rub chicken breasts with olive oil and sprinkle both sides with salt and creole seasonings. Put in a boiler pan.
  3. Leave in oven for 10 minutes and flip, leave for another 15 minutes.
  4. Remove from oven and move chicken to plate and serve.
