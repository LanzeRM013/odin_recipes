Banana Foster

Back to Homepage

Bananas Foster is made from bananas and vanilla ice cream with sauce that consists of butter, brown sugar, cinnamon, and rum(an alchoholic drink). Orginating from a restaurant in New Orleans 1951, it is usually prepared and served as a dessert where alcohol is added and ignited.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Rum
  • Vanilla extracts
  • Cinnamon
  • Bananas
  • Nuts
  • Ice cream

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Melt butter in a large, deep skillet in medium heat.
  3. Put in and stir brown sugar, rum, vanilla and cinnamon; bring to a low boil
  4. Place bananas and walnuts in the pan. Cook until softened, 1 to 2 minutes.
  5. Serve at once over vanilla ice cream. Done!
