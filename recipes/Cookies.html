Chocolate Chips Cookies

Back to Homepage

The most popular of all cookies, a classic. These soft, chewy and irresistable cookies are easy to make in just 9 easy steps.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Sugar(white and brown)
  • Vanilla extract
  • Baking soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate chips
  • Nuts(if desired)

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients. Make sure butter is softened and eggs in room temperature
  2. Pre-heat oven to 350 F(175 C). Beat butter, white sugar and brown sugar in a large bowl until smooth
  3. Beat eggs one at a time, then add and stir in vanilla
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water, then add to batter along with salt
  5. Stir in flour, chocolate chips and nuts(optional)
  6. Drop spoonful of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets
  7. Bake in oven for 10 minutes, until edges are brown
  8. Remove from oven and let cool
  9. Store in airtight container or serve immediately. Enjoy!
